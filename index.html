About ComURide

ComURide is an application developed to solve the rush hour traffic congestions in Lagos state and beyond. It is built on the power of ride sharing by people with common interests and goals. ComURide was created to disrupt the way people (especially workers) go to and fro work on daily basis through useful connections of people with similar interests and likes. Learn more

How Does it Work?

You can sign up as a car owner (driver) or rider. During a trip, you can either be a car owner or rider. The rider’s status can only change to car owner (driver) in a trip when he/she is using his/her car.

1. Connect

Connect to friends, co-workers, neighbours and people of similar interests, goals and destinations.

2. Contact

‘’YOLLA’’ the Driver to indicate interest.
Driver accepts the yolla

3. Come, ride with me

Driver picks up the riders at the pre-agreed spot.
Driver drops off the riders at the agreed destination.
